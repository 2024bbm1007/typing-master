# Optimizing Typing Ergonomics for Developers

Good ergonomics reduce fatigue and increase speed. Follow these tips while using the **Lessons** page:

- Adjust your chair and monitor height.
- Keep wrists straight and use a light touch.
- Take short breaks every 20 minutes.

Learn more about tracking posture metrics in the **Technical Docs**.
